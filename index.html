Welcome !

Monuments crafted from locally found natural materials

I am an outdoor sculpture artist weaving fallen leaves, interlocking fractured twigs, and balancing river stones using only gravity and water. Every installation is built by hand, documented in natural light, and left to return gently to the landscape.
